Soak up sun, sea and sand

The sun shines throughout the year in Qatar, which makes it the ideal destination for a beach holiday.

From popular city beaches to hidden gems, there is an abundance of beautiful beaches across the country. Qatar enjoys pleasant weather all year long and is ideal for sun-seekers and those who want to refuel and rejuvenate. Explore Qatar's beaches and islands, embark on relaxed activities under the sun, choose from various hotels and resorts for your perfect vacation, plan the ideal getaway with family or friends, and encounter some wildlife.
